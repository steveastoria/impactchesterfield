Welcome

Welcome to Impact Chesterfield Inc. We are a 501 (c) (3) nonprofit organization helping homeless students and their families in Chesterfield County, VA, through School Resource Closets, Material Needs, and Rent Security Deposit Assistance since 2022.

Our Mission

Empowers families experiencing homelessness in Chesterfield County, VA by providing comprehensive support and resources to foster stability and self-sufficiency.

Our Programs

  • Emergency shelter placement assistance
  • Job readiness and employment support
  • Financial literacy workshops
  • Family counseling and mentoring
  • Partnerships with local housing providers
