Welcome

Welcome to Impact Chesterfield Inc. We are a 501 (c) (3) nonprofit organization helping homeless students and their families in Chesterfield County, VA, through School Resource Closets, Material Needs, Temporary Lodging, and Rent Security Deposit Assistance since 2022.

Our Mission

Empowers families experiencing homelessness in Chesterfield County, VA by providing comprehensive support and resources to foster stability and self-sufficiency.

Our Programs

  • Resource Closets - We have established Resource Closets in Harrowgate Elementary School, Falling Creek Elementary School, and Manchester Middle School. The Resource Closets are cabinets that usually sit in the Community-In-School Coordinator's office. Impact Chesterfield fills and refills the cabinets and Community-In-Schools empties them. About 85 students use the Resource Closets. Closets are refiled between once and twice a month.
  • Material Needs - We recieve requests from the Chesterfield County Public School (CCPS) McKinney-Vento Director seeking items homeless students need, such as clothing, beds and mattresses, tables, lamps, coats, food, etc. We have supplied items by canvassing members for used items or donations.
  • Temporary Lodging - When families loose their permanent residence, many times famlies need a few days lodging to resettle themselves. We received requestd from the CCPS McKinney-Vento Director and we provide 1 to 5 days of lodging in a local hotel.
